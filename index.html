PRESENT CONTINUOUS : SCRIPT RULES

AFFIRMATIVE

Sujet + am / is / are + Verbe-ing
Ex: I am playing / He is running.

NEGATIVE

Sujet + am not / isn't / aren't + Verbe-ing
Ex: They aren't watching TV.

INTERROGATIVE

Am / Is / Are + sujet + Verbe-ing ?
Ex: Are you listening to me?

ORTHOGRAPHE (-ING)

• Verbe en -e ➔ Effacez le -e (write ➔ writing)
• Verbe court CVC ➔ Doublez la consonne (run ➔ running)
• Verbe en -ie ➔ Devient -ying (die ➔ dying)
